# Chuckle-Mate---Support
Chuckle Mate Support
Thanks for using Chuckle Mate! We’re here to help you get the most out of your "Giggle Level" challenges.

FAQ
Q: Why does the app need camera access?
A: Chuckle Mate uses ARKit to track your facial expressions for the game mechanics. Without camera access, we can't score your funny faces!

Q: The game isn't tracking my face properly.
A: Ensure you are in a well-lit environment and your face is clearly visible to the front-facing camera. For the best experience, use a device with a TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later).

Q: Where are my captured photos saved?
A: Photos are only saved to your local Photos App if you tap the "Save to Photos" button after a successful challenge.
